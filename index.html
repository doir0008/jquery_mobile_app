Two bucket wash method

About this App

This app is intended to explain the "Two bucket wash method" used to properly wash a car and keep its exterior properly cleared and maintained.

The "two bucket wash method" uses two wash buckets, as indicated, to separate clean and dirty water, minimizing any possible damage that may be caused to the coats of paint on your car. It is highly recommended using this method if you care to keep your car in good-condition.
