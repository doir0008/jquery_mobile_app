Two bucket wash

Want to keep that new car look?

Well you're in luck! This app will help by demonstrating the two bucket wash method.

  • Begin Tutorial
  • Advanced Exterior Cleaning Tips

The two bucket wash method uses two wash buckets, as indicated, to separate clean and dirty water, minimizing any possible damage that may be caused to the protective layers of paint on your car. It is highly recommended using this method if you care to keep your car looking show-room ready.

About the Developer

My name is Ryan Doiron and I am the developer of this app. I love programming and am currently a first year student in the Mobile Application Design & Development program at Algonquin College.

I love my Mini Cooper S, I bought it back in 2014 and it is the first new car I have ever owned. I intent to keep it well maintained and I thought it would be nice to share some information on how I just do that through the two bucket wash method.
